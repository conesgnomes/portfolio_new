about me

I am an inquisitive person. I ask a lot of questions in an effort to understand and connect with the people and places around me. I approach my design process with the same curiosity. By seeking to understand first, I can better ensure that my designs connect with their target audience.

Who I am

Hailing from rainy Portland, Oregon, I am a creative individual who believes inspiration can be found in the most unlikely of places. Aside from funding life’s necessities, I make money so that I can travel. I believe experiencing the world, tasting its delicious foods, and observing its beautiful art, architecture and nature is what it is all about.
